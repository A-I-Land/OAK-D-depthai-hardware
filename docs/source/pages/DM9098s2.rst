Overview
********

The **OAK-D S2** is the series 2 of the :ref:`OAK-D`. The main difference is that
OAK-D S2 has a smaller enclosure and is therefore lighter. OAK-D S2 also doesn't have a barrel jack
for supplying power, so it only has a USB-C connector for both power and communication.
